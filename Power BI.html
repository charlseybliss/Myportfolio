Brazillian E-commerce Analysis

Title: BRAZILLIAN E-commerce SALES ANALYSIS: OLIST (2016 — 2018)

Description: This project is a visualization of transaction data from OLIST. OLIST is the largest department store in Brazil. The Dataset contains record of over 100k orders from 2016 to 2018 made at multiple market place in Brazil with insights into monthly Orders, customers' payment preference, behavior and product category performance.

Data Source: This Dataset was provided by Quantum Analytics NG.

Key Metrics:
* Gross Revenue : $20M
* City : 4119
* Orders : 118k
* Total Sales Persons : 3096
* Total Customers : 99k
* State : 26

Key Insights:

*The total Gross Revenue is 20M from 118K orders across 26 states with 99K customers.
*3096 sellers are contributing to this revenue.
São Paulo leads in both customer distribution and orders, followed by Rio de Janeiro and Minas Gerais. São Paulo also dominates in city-specific distribution.
*Orders peak in August but decline steadily throughout the year, with the lowest in December.
*Orders decrease significantly from Monday to Saturday, with Monday being the busiest and Saturday the least active.
*The majority of ratings are 5 stars, followed by 4 stars. Low ratings (1 or 2 stars) are minimal.
*Credit cards dominate as the preferred payment method, followed by debit cards and vouchers.
*"Bed, Bath & Table" is the leading category, followed by "Health & Beauty" and "Sports & Leisure". Categories like "Garden Tools" and "Auto" generate the least revenue.
Orders declined significantly in 2016.

Recommendations:

*Focus marketing campaigns on underperforming states like Goiás, Espírito Santo, and others to balance the geographic revenue distribution.
*Explore promotions and discounts for Friday and Saturday to boost sales on these low-performing days.
*Categories like Garden Tools and Auto could benefit from bundled offers or seasonal promotions to increase revenue.
*Promote products with consistently 5-star reviews in advertising to build trust and attract more customers.
*Investigate the cause of the steep decline in orders in 2016. Potential reasons could include increased competition, changes in consumer behavior, or economic factors.
*Since credit cards are the dominant payment method, ensure smooth and secure payment processes to reduce cart abandonment rates.
*Capitalize on months like August with promotional campaigns to sustain or exceed the peak sales levels.
*Invest in targeted marketing for top-performing categories such as Bed, Bath & Table, Health & Beauty, and Sports & Leisure, while identifying additional cross-selling opportunities.

Flight Cancellation And Depature Delay Report

Title - Flight Cancelation And Delay Report (2015)

This project is an Analysis of flight cancellation and delay recorded from 5,000,000+ US commercial airlines in the year 2015. Each record represents a single flight, including the airline name, flight number, origin/destination airport and flight distance, as well as scheduled/actual departure and arrival times.

Data Source: This Dataset was provided by Quantum Analytics NG.

Key Metrics
* Total flights: 6M (Approximately)
* % of Delayed Depature Time : 37%
* Cancelled Flight: 90k
* Total Airlines: 14
* Average Delayed Depature Time: 33 Minutes
* No. of Airports: 323
* % Of Missing Depature Delay: 1% (Depature time not provided)
* % of On Time Depature: 6%
* % of Undelayed Depature: 56%
Objectives
The Objective of this analysis is to uncover the airlines whose depature delay time, flight Cancellation and arrival delay time are at minumum and be able to recommend the best Airlines to think of when the idea of travelling comes up And also to provide recommendation to reduce Depature Delay and arrival delay time.

Trends and insights
* Monthly flight volume was observed to have maxed on July with a total flight of 520,718 while the least was on February recording 429,191 flights.
* It was seen that the major cause of Flight Cancellation was weather condition which contributed 66% to overall flight cancellation when compared to Flight cancelled by Airlines been 34%.
* Southwest Airline ranked first as the most reliable Airline by depature time, having total undelayed flight of 587,783, followed by Delta airlines and American Airlines.
* The least reliable airline is Virgin America with total undelayed flight of 34,123.
Recommendation
*Use advanced weather forecasting tools to better anticipate severe weather impacts and proactively reschedule flights to reduce flight cancellation, mitigate weather-related delays and allow for better contingency planning and thus, improving customer satisfaction
*Work closely with airports and air traffic control to better align schedulling and reduce bottlenecks during peak hours which will help improve ontime depatures by ensuring smoother coordination between all operational touchpoints.
