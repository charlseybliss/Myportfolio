Sales Analysis Dashboard

Title : EXCEL INTERACTIVE SALES DASHBOARD
Description: This Dataset contains records of sales of an unspecified set of items, represented by item(1,2,3,4 and 5).
Data Source: This dataset was provided by Quantum Analytics

Tool used: Microsoft Excel

Objective: The goal of this analysis is to uncover the top selling items, identify those items that are in high demand, recognize the top sales persons and be able to replicate the strategy used to improve areas with lower performance.

Key Metrics:
* Revenue : $2,028,591.00
* Average Revenue: $1,014,36.00
* Total Orders : 2000
* Distinct items : 5
* Quantity Sold: 9119
* Sales Persons: 8
* Total Region : 4

Trends And insights:
* Texas contributed the most Revenue with $532,135 (26%) while the least was from New Mexico with $492,984 (24.3%).
* item 2 and 4 are in high demand with 21% contribution respectively to over all demand.
* item 1 and 5 contributed 19% to the over all orders
* March had the peak monthly Revenue Contribution while the least was on December due to holiday period.
•2018 has the highest Revenue Contribution
* item 1 had the highest contribution to Revenue with sum of $736,953, while the least was item 3 with $124,890 contribution.
•Based on sales persons the highest Revenue was contributed by Anna Weber with sum of $276,378.

filters: you can filter this visualization by month to dig more into the monthly trend.
