SPOTIFY TRACK ANALYSIS

Title - SPOTIFY TRACK ANALYSIS

This project is an analysis of Unicorn companies which in Venture capital industries is list of privately held companies that ar valued at over a Billion dollars. The dataset contains 1000+ companies which are valued at over a billion dollar.

Data Source: Kaggle
Tools used: Excel

KPI:
Average Popularity: 30.75
Total Artists: 4649
Average Duration : 3 minute
Tracks: 4364
Explicit Tracks: 19%
Maximum Duration: 60 minute

Observations:
popularity Trend:

• The track - Houdini by Eminem was the most popular track with average popularity score of 90.
• Rock ranked first as the most popular genre with average popularity score of 60.34
• Based on Explicit contents, Pagode ranked first as the most popular genre with popularity score of 66.
• Central Cee and Lil Baby collaboration are the most popular Artists based on popularity score with popularity - 87, but in terms of single artist Eminem is the most popular with average popularity score of 90.

Genre Performance

• it was observed that some top 5 popular genre had the most Track count which could have contributed to it's popularity and it also shows audience preference for these genres. Track duration

• songs between duration range 2-4 minute tend to have high popularity showing indicating audience preference for concise songs.

explicit tracks

• The proportion of explicit tracks vary significantly across genre, and it's mostly dominated by non- explicit track. the overall percentage of explicit track is 19%.

Recommendation

1. popular tracks like Rock, dance,country etc should be promoted as it reveals great level of audience preference.
2. For explicit content, Pagode can be targeted for marketing campaigns as it ranks highest in popularity for explicit tracks.
3. Aim to produce tracks with a duration of 2-4 minutes, as these tend to perform better in terms of audience engagement and popularity.
4. Eminem’s single-artist popularity can be used to drive campaigns or collaborations.
5. The collaboration between Central Cee and Lil Baby shows promise for high engagement and should be leveraged in promotions or future collaborations.
